Account & Data Deletion – BhuMitra

BhuMitra provides an in-app option to delete user accounts and associated data.

How to delete your account:

  1. Open the BhuMitra app
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Go to Profile
  4. Click on "Delete Account"
  5. Enter your password
  6. Click on Confirm to delete

Once confirmed, your account and associated data (email, phone number, and location-related data) will be permanently deleted.
